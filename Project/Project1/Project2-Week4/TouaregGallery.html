Connected for perfect control

With its expansive 15-inch touchscreen, this "one-screen design" seamlessly integrates navigation, connectivity, and entertainment into a single digital landscape. Boasting a 1900 x 1,020 pixels resolution, it provides crisp graphics and easy accessibility to all features.

Wireless App-Connect® allows you to easily display selected Apple or Android smartphone apps on the infotainment touchscreen system via the Apple CarPlay™ and Android Auto™, respectively. Additionally, the system includes a 45-watt USB-C connection for rapid device charging and offers wireless charging for added convenience.

Limitless power, within reach

The 3.0 V6 TSI 340PS engine at the core of the Touareg R-Line offers a driving experience that's both powerful and polished. With 450 Nm of torque, it delivers instant acceleration and effortless power on demand.

Each shift in the Touareg feels finely tuned to deliver power efficiently, thanks to its 8-Speed automatic transmission. Whether you're accelerating on a highway or navigating through city traffic, it ensures a dynamic ride that is both exhilarating and efficient.

The Touareg 4MOTION all-wheel drive system defines stability and control, offering unparalleled performance in various driving conditions. It continuously monitors and adjusts the power distribution between the front and rear wheels to ensure optimal traction and handling on the road.

Designed to keep you safer

No matter what kind of road you're driving on, if you're 35 km/h or faster, IQ.DRIVE can help keep you from going over the lines. If your vehicle begins to drift without your turn signal on, IQ.DRIVE can warn you and counter-steer when activated to help ensure you stay in your lane.

Navigate uncertain exits and tricky junctions with Front Cross Traffic Assist. This system provides acoustic and visual warnings at speeds up to 30 km/h, using radar technology to detect crossing vehicles. In critical situations, it can even initiate emergency braking at speeds up to 10 km/h.
